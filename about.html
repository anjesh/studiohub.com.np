About Studio Hub

Thoughtful spaces shaped from concept to construction.

Founded by architects Deepika Tuladhar and Liza Pal, the studio works across architecture, interiors, visualization, drawing coordination, and project delivery. Their work combines design development with technical detailing and practical execution across a wide range of building types.

About Co-Founders

Before establishing Studio Hub Consulting in 2021, both founders worked at Design Cell Pvt. Ltd., one of Nepal's reputed architecture firms, where they contributed to major design projects as principal architects. That experience continues to shape the studio's approach to design quality, technical coordination, and project execution.

Their combined background spans hospitality, resort, commercial, corporate interior, healthcare, and recreation-linked developments. Across these sectors, they have led preliminary design, detailed drawing, interior design, 3D visualization, and coordination with structural, sanitary, electrical, mechanical, and construction teams.

In hospitality and resort work, their experience includes projects such as Rupakot Resort at Pokhara, Temple Tree Resort at Pokhara, Barahi Hotel, and Mountain Glory Forest Resort. In specialized and mixed-use developments, they have also worked on projects such as the Interior Design of the Presidential Suite at Hotel Radisson, Pathibhara Cable Car, Lumbini Cable Car, Maulakali Cable Car, and Kundalini Diagnostic Center.

Studio Hub Consulting builds on that foundation by offering architecture, interior design, technical drawing, visualization, and project support with a focus on clear design thinking and practical delivery.
